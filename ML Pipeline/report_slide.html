Experimentation Details & Configurations

  • All experiments were conducted on a High-Performance Computing (HPC) cluster.
  • Epochs: 40 epochs were used for the AGCRN model, while 50 epochs were used for DSTAGNN, GraphWaveNet, MLP, and TCN.
  • Model Sources & Configurations:
    • AGCRN: Based on the paper by Bai et al.
    • DSTAGNN: Based on the paper by Lan et al.
    • GraphWaveNet: Utilized the vanilla implementation from its original repository.
    • HSDGNN: Based on the paper "Graph Neural Networks for multivariate time-series forecasting via learning hierarchical spatiotemporal dependencies" by Zhou et al.
    • MLP: Configuration inspired by the work of Niska et al. on forecasting air pollution time series.
    • TCN: Configuration influenced by the paper "Leveraging Hybrid Deep Learning Models for Enhanced Multivariate Time Series Forecasting" by Mahmoud and Mohammed.
